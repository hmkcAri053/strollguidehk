Routes Informations

Starting from Sung Wong Toi, wander through the heritage-filled streets of Kowloon City toward the tranquil Kowloon Walled City Park, where traces of Hong Kong’s layered history meet local life. Across the harbor in North Point, begin at Kam Ping Mansion and pass parks, churches, and quiet neighborhoods that reveal the city’s everyday rhythm — locals chatting in tea houses, students in motion, elders strolling under morning light — together forming a calm, authentic portrait of Hong Kong.

Food Guide

“Eating alone” isn’t loneliness — it’s a quiet way to connect with the city. Along each walking route, I’ve noted hidden eateries perfect for one: a wonton noodle shop tucked in an alley, a dessert café under an old apartment, or a small table by the window of a cozy coffee shop. Sitting alone, you taste not just the food but the soul of the city — one bite at a time.
